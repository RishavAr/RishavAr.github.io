### 🛡️ Overview
Designed and implemented **NetGuard**, a serverless multi-agent cybersecurity platform powered by SecureGPT and AWS.

### 📊 Results
- **94.5% accuracy**, **90.8% F1-score** on real-time network threat detection  
- Validated across **6,000+ traffic samples** with low-latency inference  

### 🧠 Architecture
- Modular agents: Ingestor, Analyzer, Aggregator, Ticket Generator  
- SecureGPT-based reasoning with **NIST-compliant ticket generation**
